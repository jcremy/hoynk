# HOYNK

**Hold On! You Never Know.**

A habit with a name. A practice of keeping the parts, before the whole thing goes.

**👉 Read the page: [hoynk.issify.com](https://hoynk.issify.com/)**

---

## What is HOYNK?

HOYNK names a specific practice: **keeping parts of things you'd otherwise throw away, because some future repair or build will need exactly that part.**

The broken chair leg becomes tomorrow's wall hook. The scrap of fabric patches the worn-out bag. The hinge in the drawer fits a door you haven't even hung yet.

It's not upcycling, not reuse, not second-hand, not recycling, not ecology, not hoarding. Each of those is its own thing. HOYNK names a sixth move that was hiding inside the "sustainability" conversation without a word of its own.

## What's in this repo?

A single static page — the one at [hoynk.issify.com](https://hoynk.issify.com/). Plain HTML, CSS, a few lines of JavaScript. **No app, no framework, no build step.**

If you landed here expecting software, there isn't any. HOYNK is a concept; this page is how it travels. Fork it, translate it, host your own copy.
